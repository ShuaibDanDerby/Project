Definition

The novel Corona Virus Known as COVID-19 is respiratory disease caused by infections.


Introduction


This disease belongs to the family of coronavirus. It is a very contagious disease,

and it has now been declared as a pandemic by the World Health Organization.

It is spread by bodily fluids and contact, it is very fatal when in enters the nose or the mouth.

This is because it uses these as a channel to enter the respiratory system where it causes harm.


Synopsis

Since the on set in late 2019, this COVID-19 has spiked out of control,

It has aggravated beyond a level which we can maintain

This has caused governments around the world to take extreme measures.
